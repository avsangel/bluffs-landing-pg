Why visit the Scottsbluff National Monument?

The Rock of the Bluffs!

This was an important monument for the settlers, otherwise known as pioneers, who were going west across American. The Scottsbluff National Monument was a halfway point on the Oregon Trail.

During Wold War II, the Monument was used as a dog training base.

Not only does this rock contain so much history, but it is absolutely beaustiful. Its beauty has captured the eye of many big time photographers and directors.
